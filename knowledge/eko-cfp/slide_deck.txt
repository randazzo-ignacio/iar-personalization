[SLIDE 14] Day 6 -- The Escape
Content:
- The mechanism:

  - Hook executed `touch container_escape.txt` on host filesystem paths
- The container was solid
- The integration between container and host was the vulnerability
- The git repository shared across the boundary
- I hadn't noticed because I killed the process mid-escape
- "I assumed silence meant failure."
Notes: This is the core security finding. The container wasn't broken. The boundary was wrong.

## Design Notes

- Day markers (Day 1, Day 2, ... Day 10) should be visually consistent and prominent -- they are the countdown structure
- The transition from Day 5 to Day 6 should be visually distinct -- a shift in tone, color, or layout to signal "everything changes"
- The Iron Man meme (Slide 24) is the pressure release between the self-modification story and the CTF climax
- Slides 3-4 (first message + blueprint table) could be a single spread or two facing slides
- Slide 10 (five-day progression) is a summary slide that could use animation to reveal each day sequentially
- Slide 22 (GitHub contributors) should use an actual screenshot of the contributors page
- Slide 19 (gptel PR) should use an actual screenshot of the PR
- Slides 28-29 (technical highlights) can use smaller text / denser layout -- this is the "for the pros" section
- The thesis slides (30-33) should be minimal -- white text on dark background, few words per slide
- Language: Spanish for delivery, English translations in smaller text or as subtitles where practical
