# ekoparty 2025 CFP Guidelines

## Event Overview

Ekoparty is back! More than 10.000 hackers from all over Latin America and the world will get together at the major meeting point for the cybersecurity community in the region.

Now in its 22nd year, Ekoparty celebrates his conference at the stunning Centro de Convenciones de Buenos Aires again. The event will open with six days of advanced cybersecurity Training with hands-on courses for all audiences. The three-day leading conference (October 7, 8, 9) will feature more than 150 selected talks, thematic Villages and challenges, an important Business Hall, networking and social events, and much more!

## Keys to Be Accepted

- Submissions without a proper outline, detailed description and complete biography of the author will be rejected.
- Preference will be given to presentations with attached technical documents and a slide deck.
- Special consideration will be given to ORIGINAL work and content that has not been presented in any other event before.
- You can submit and deliver your talk in english or spanish. Puedes enviar y presentar tu charla en español o inglés.
